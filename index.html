ABOUT Pancali Cottage

Pancali Cottage is set in Lembongan. Offering an outdoor pool, Bali Nusa Cottage is a brand new property set in Lembongan. The Mushroom bay is 100 meters from the property. Free WiFi is provided . Featuring a terrace, all units are air conditioned and include a seating area. There is a private clean bathroom in every unit. Towels are available. Guests can enjoy breakfast at the on-site restaurant. Guests can enjoy various activities in the surroundings, including snorkeling and diving. The nearest airport is Ngurah Rai International Airport, about 50 km from the property.
